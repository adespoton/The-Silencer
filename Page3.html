Coalition Walkthrough

  1. Find a Militia soldier and Navy officer in the woods south of Gravnotton. They will be near a mysterious building. This is the start of the Coalition storyline, and allows the player to start doing issions for the Navy.
  2. Go to Duale, and find a Navy officer on the docks. Talk to him and he will give you a bandit-hunting mission and a letter.
  3. Go SE and meet a BRD division. Return to Duale and meet with Colonel Saltsmith again.
  4. Go find a Naval officer between Gravnotton and Nosund and give him a message. He will tell you to return to Duale.
  5. Return to Duale and join the Navy.
  6. Go to Nosund to check for bandits in then return to Duale.

The Coalition storyline is as of yet unfinished and the above steps are as far as you can get in it.
